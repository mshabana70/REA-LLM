Friends Collect Gems - control two characters at the same time to collect as many gems as you can. Watch out for the enemy squares!

Friends Collect Gems is a simple game with a unique twist. Control two friend characters simultaneously to collect gems while avoiding the enemy squares. As time goes on, difficulty increases providing a challenge to the player. Quick reflexes are required to survive long! High scores are saved to Google Play Games leaderboards.

Many characters are available to unlock!🔓 Collect gems💎 to earn coins to spend on new friend characters to play as.

HOW TO PLAY:

-Touch the left-side of the screen to control the left-side character.⬅

-Touch the right-side of the screen to control the right-side character.➡

-Collect the gems💎 and avoid the enemy squares.🟦🟥

-Survive as long as you can!⏲
